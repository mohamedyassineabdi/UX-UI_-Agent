Verbs are used for all actions (e.g. Save, Go, Submit, Continue).

Issue: On Accueil, the audit found that 'Verbs are used for all actions (e.g. Save, Go, Submit, Continue).' is not fully met. Action labels do not pass the verb criterion. At least one page relies too heavily on non-verb action labels.

Why it matters: This matters because recurring patterns do not behave or look consistently, which makes the product feel less mature. In a GTM context, visible friction on Accueil can make the product feel harder to understand, trust, or adopt during a first review.

Direction: Prefer direct verb-led action labels such as Search, Add to cart, Continue, Save, or Send.
